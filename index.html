About CSI

CSI is a Cybersecurity club based in ENSI, National School of Computer Sciences - Tunisia. It's an open community for all cybersecurity enthusiasts.

The club's main purpose is to educate students and raise their awareness about cybersecurity within a friendly and interactive atmosphere.

We provide free, diversified cybersecurity trainings and we organize CTF competitions and hackathons to challenge students, to develop their teamwork abilities and to sharpen their cybersecurity skills.

HackZone IX

Hack Zone is one of the greatest cybersecurity events in Tunisia. Since 2013, the annual meet-up aims to convene students, cybersecurity enthusiasts and professionals under one roof to create an interactive atmosphere for the exchange of knowledge and ideas.
